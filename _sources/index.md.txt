Welcome to the Panel Discussion on Tools & Best-Practices!
==========================================================

Panel discussion as part of the [Electronic Structure Software Development][essd]
workshop hosted by the [Centre Européen de Calcul Atomique et Moléculaire][cecam].

- **Where:** Zoom - see invitation for link
- **When:** October 10th, 16:00 - 17:30 CEST

The purpose of this website is to serve as a collection of potential topics
for seeding the discussion on tools & best practices for open-source
scientific software development.
Of course, if there is a topic you'd like to discuss that isn't mentioned here,
please don't hesitate to bring it up!

This information is also viewable as a slide show --- see the
